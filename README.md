
​Project Overview
​This project features an interactive sales dashboard built in Microsoft Excel. It centralizes data from various regions (Central, East, South, West) to provide a 360-degree view of sales health and achievement rates from 2021 through 2024.

​Plan Achievement Rate: A gauge chart tracks overall progress against set targets, currently showing a high achievement rate of 92%.
​Regional Performance: Interactive slicers allow for deep-dives into specific regions and years (2021, 2022, 2023, and 2024).
​Top Cities by Revenue: A ranked list identifies the highest-revenue-generating cities, led by New York City (3,07,642) and Los Angeles (2,11,021).
​Product Sub-category Breakdown: A pie chart illustrates the percentage of sales contributed by different product categories, such as Phones (18%), Storage (13%), and Tables (12%).
​Temporal Trends: Bar charts visualize revenue trends across fiscal quarters and years, facilitating year-over-year (YoY) comparisons.# Sales_excel_Dashboard
This Sales Performance Dashboard provides a multi-year analysis of regional sales metrics, target achievements, and product category distributions. It is designed to help sales managers track performance against goals and identify top-performing markets.
